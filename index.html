About us

ZingLEES (Zing Learner Evaluation & Empowerment Solutions) is proprietary offering of Brain Scale Innovations designed and developed to support teachers and schools in overcoming their practical challenges in the process of ensuring the learning for every child in their classroom. ZingLEES supports teachers and schools in moving from the current practice of examination systems to a more purposeful evaluation systems. ZingLEES also enables the teachers and schools to address and enable every student according to their specific needs, strengths and weaknesses.

Who are we

Zinglees partners with parents and schools all over India to facilitate their understanding of how children learn especially post primary classes. We act as an independent evaluation partner, focused primarily upon providing diagnostic reports of each child by which we will be able to give every child the unparalleled attention that they deserve.
