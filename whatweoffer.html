What we offer

Through our services, we aim at enabling schools, teachers, and parents to get access to complete insights on each child to give them the special care and attention that they truly need. We're revamping the entire educational assessment system to the specific needs of each child to ensure that they get the most out of their education.

  • ZingLEES

    • In this exponentially changing times in the knowledge based economy, building competent and confident students become the key responsibility of the schools. In other words, the new deliverable of the schools are to ensure the required level of learning for all the students so that they are confident in exhibiting their confidence.
    • If we consider ‘Learning Outcomes’ as the re-defined deliverable of the school, then we have to re-engineer the pedagogy to meet the purpose of the schools and expectations of the parents.
    • ZingLEES’s a proprietary intelligence platform of Brain Scale, helps teachers transform from teaching all children to teaching every child in their classroom.
    • ZingLEES’s helps teachers to understand the varied learning levels of every student in every concept in every subject. This detailed diagnose helps teachers to customize the prescription to suit the need of every learner.
    • ZingLEES’s offering helps schools in achieving their founding purpose.

  • Scorerz

    • Irrespective of the amount of effort "Practice makes a person perfect" Right? Well, we follow it to the letter. In scorerz we have tens of thousands of questions for the students to have repeated flexible practice.
    • These flexible practice platforms, help students in creating practice tests within seconds in subjects and chapters of their choice. Students have option to choose the duration as well.
    • The detailed analytical reports give the students and teachers more clarity on the distinguished needs of each of the students in the class to address and take more practice on those specific subjects or chapters.
    • Don't miss out on the opportunity to get your hands on these amazing practice materials that have helped thousands of students succeed.
