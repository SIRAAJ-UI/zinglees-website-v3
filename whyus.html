Why us

If we are able to have a deeper understanding and answer to the question ‘why schools?’ we will be able to understand the relevance of ‘why Zinglees?’

Schools and teachers

Schools and teachers have a huge responsibility of grooming, competent and compassionate individuals who are our hopes for tomorrow. Except for a small insignificant number, a vast majority of the parent community pin their hopes on schools for bettering their wards. It is a big challenge for the schools to prepare their children in exponentially changing times.Every country has started laying its focus on defining and stressing on the age appropriate learning outcomes at the school level.

Learning

With learning becoming the new deliverable of the school, we have tweak our existing to pedagogy to ensure the learning of the students and this is where Zinglees’s offering supports teachers and schools to support children. Learning is the deliverable of the school. Schools are founded not just to teach but to ensure learning of the children. Now that answers our initial question of ‘why schools?’ ‘Teaching’ is a process and ‘Learning’ the purpose of the school.

Zinglees’s offering

Zinglees’s proprietary intelligence platforms helps teachers transform from teaching all children to teaching every child in their classroom. Zinglees’s offering helps teachers to understand the varied learning levels of every student in every concept in every subject. This detailed diagnose helps teachers to customize the prescription to suit the need of every learner. Zinglees’s offering helps schools in achieving their founding purpose.
